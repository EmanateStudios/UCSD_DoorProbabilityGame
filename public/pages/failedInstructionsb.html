Game Instructions

Please read the game instructions below carefully as there will be a quiz before the game begins.

Thanks for participating in this study. You will play a very simple video game for about 60 minutes.


Once you start, you cannot defocus the window or do anything else (like check your email) or you will be disqualified and not be credited for participation.





On each play, you will be shown 3 doors. You will open one of the doors by clicking on the door. If there is a treasure chest behind the door you just opened, you will gain 20 points. If instead there is rubble, you will lose 10 points.


Your goal is to open as many doors with treasure chests as you can.


Getting a good score is not pure luck, and it is not just a matter of how you pick which door to open. It may also be how you open the door.


The game has 6 rounds each with 20 plays. Between each round you will have a 20 second rest period. Remember that you cannot defocus the window during the game including these rest periods.


We need your full concentration throughout this experiment. Please try your best! Thanks for participating, and have fun!
